Miso Soup Recipe

Ingredients
  • Tofu
  • Well-fermented Kimchi
  • Anchovies
  • Daikon Radish
  • Green Onion
  • Sesame Seeds
    1. Bring water to a rolling boil
    2. Add Anchovies, daikon radish, kimchi and green onions
    3. Keep at a boil for at least 20 minutes
    4. Add tofu and boil for 5 minutes
    5. Top with green onions and sesame seeds and serve!
